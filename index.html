About the Author

Abraham Oyedele

Abraham Oyedele is a called minister of the gospel whose pastoral assignments in praying, counseling and deliverance spans the length and breadth of the home country Nigeria and extending to the west coast. His wealth of experience along this line has birthed two books "The Power of a Wombman" and "Youths in Their Time" amongst other projects developed for Television and film audience with the passion to get more of kingdom candidates involved in the art of character and career moulding.

He is the producer and convener of a youth based Television programme, "Youth Engage" which primarily is about reorienting the youth for a better tomorrow.

His company Abraham Oyedele Productions Limited has been involved in productions of TVCs, documentaries for notable establishments like Doyin Group and Olusegun Obasanjo Presidential library amongst others. An Alumnus of the Institute of Mass Communication Technology, Jos, Nigeria with mandate to bring sanity to as many homes through Christ inspired qualitative programmes both on pulpit and the screen.
